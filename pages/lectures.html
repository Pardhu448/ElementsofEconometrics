Following is the general pattern for covering each topic :

  • Friday (3 hrs) : Introduce topic and cover major part of it. Assignment- prepare five questions each from text and slides, attempt three excercise questions from the topics covered.
  • Tuesday (2 hrs): Continue with some more portion of topic and keep an hour for discussion of last week assignment.
  • Friday (3 hrs): Complete the chapter. Assignment- prepare three questions from slides and two from text, attempt five past year questions and five excercise questions.
  • Tuesday (2 hrs): Review of chapter and Discussion of past questions and excercises.
