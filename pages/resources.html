This page contains all the additional resources relevant for introductory econometrics course. Resources are added as and when they are relevant for the topics covered in the course.

Optional Online Course: Best introductory courses to understand the practical aspects of econometrics and causal reasoning in statistics is "Foundations of Data Science" . They teach inference and computing at introductory level and is very popular among undergraduate students at UC Berkley. This course can be done simultaneously with intrductory econometrics course as the concepts taught are similar, only that data science course is more practical.

Online Textbook Resources: Textbook closely follows syllabus for 'Elements of Econometrics' course. This is page contains presentations and data sets for different topics of course.
